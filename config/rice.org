* Rice

Visual configuration: theme, fonts, modeline, and cosmetic tweaks.

** Modus Themes

The Modus themes are Emacs' built-in accessible themes. The dark variant
(=modus-vivendi-tinted=) is loaded by default. Toggle between light and
dark with =C-c m m=.

Custom styling: accented borderless mode line, tinted org block
backgrounds, rainbow headings that scale by level, intense paren
matching and prompt highlighting.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(setq modus-themes-mode-line '(accented borderless)
      modus-themes-bold-constructs t
      modus-themes-italic-constructs t
      modus-themes-fringes 'subtle
      modus-themes-tabs-accented t
      modus-themes-paren-match '(bold intense)
      modus-themes-prompts '(bold intense)
      modus-themes-completions 'opinionated
      modus-themes-org-blocks 'tinted-background
      modus-themes-scale-headings t
      modus-themes-region '(bg-only)
      modus-themes-to-toggle '(modus-operandi modus-vivendi-tritanopia)
      modus-themes-headings
      '((1 . (rainbow overline background 1.4))
        (2 . (rainbow background 1.3))
        (3 . (rainbow bold 1.2))
        (t . (semilight 1.1))))

(load-theme 'modus-vivendi-tinted)
(fringe-mode +1)
#+END_SRC

** Font

Linux default: DejavuSansMNerdFontMono at height 105 with line spacing 1.
Per-OS overrides are in =mac.org= and =windows.org=.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(set-face-attribute 'default nil
                    :family "DejavuSansMNerdFontMono"
                    :weight 'regular
                    :height 105)

(setq-default line-spacing 1)
#+END_SRC

** Doom Modeline

Rich status bar with git branch, buffer info, and notifications.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(use-package doom-modeline
  :init
  (doom-modeline-mode 1))
#+END_SRC

** Icons

Icon support for graphical frames only. Guarded with =display-graphic-p=
to avoid errors in terminal mode.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(use-package all-the-icons
  :if (display-graphic-p))
#+END_SRC

** Diminish

Hide minor modes from the modeline to reduce clutter. Currently hides
=auto-revert=, =eldoc=, and =which-key=.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(use-package diminish
  :commands diminish
  :init
  (diminish 'auto-revert-mode)
  (diminish 'eldoc-mode)
  (diminish 'which-key-mode))
#+END_SRC

** Rainbow Delimiters

Color-coded parentheses in programming modes for easier visual matching.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(use-package rainbow-delimiters
  :hook
  (prog-mode . rainbow-delimiters-mode))
#+END_SRC

** Marginalia

Rich annotations in the minibuffer (descriptions, key bindings, etc.).
Press =M-A= in the minibuffer to cycle annotation detail.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(use-package marginalia
  :bind (:map minibuffer-local-map
         ("M-A" . marginalia-cycle))
  :init
  (marginalia-mode))
#+END_SRC

** Clock

Display the current time in HH:MM format in the modeline.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(setopt display-time-format "%H:%M"
        display-time-mode t)
#+END_SRC
